## The Problem: When Meaning Collapses, People Die

Every year, thousands die not from lack of health information, but from communication that fails to make meaning across cultural and linguistic boundaries. During the 2023 Lassa Fever outbreak in Northern Nigeria, government messaging about "avoiding bush rats" backfired because it attacked a cultural food source. In UK South Asian communities during COVID-19, "protect yourself" messaging failed because it conflicted with communal obligation norms.
